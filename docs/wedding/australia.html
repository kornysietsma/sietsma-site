Directions

Mardos restaurant is in Port Melbourne, in a lovely Victorian building at 1 Crockford Street - on the corner where Bay Street, Crockford Street and Raglan Streets intersect:

You can get there by car - there should be parking in the streets nearby - or by Tram, the 109 route stops 127 is quite close by.
